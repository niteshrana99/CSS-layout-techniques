Horizontally center a block level element:

margin: 10px; => sets the margin for top,right,bottom and left;
margin: 10px 12px; => sets the margin of 10px for top,bottom and 12px for right and left;

margin:12px 20px 30px
12px=> top margin
20px=> left and right margin
30px=>bottom margin

margin:10 12 12 13;
all the four margins in order top, left, bottom, right

To center a block level element horizontally we can specify the margin: auto or margin: 0px auto; where 0px is the margin for top and bottom and auto tells us to devide the left and right margins equally after fixin the element on it's given width.
